Finally, the game.
You walk to the left, continuing on for some time. Eventually you arrive in a dark cave. You try to explore the cave, the compass necklace glints and guides your steps.
You walk confidently through the caves, until you arrive at an exit.
The exit is a portal, but it is the only escape from this place.
You take the portal.
You emerge in a parallel dimension to your own, identical in every way, except you, your family and your entire family for the last 5 generations never existed.
You wonder what the strange keyhole was about, but you never see The Labyrinth again.
The compass is your constant companion, you are never lost, and you never take off your precious.
Your paranoia about this world lends you caution and you buy a gun.
You live on for hundreds of years.
A man tries to mug you, you shoot him and flee.
You live forever, in safety, sequestered on a mountaintop.
